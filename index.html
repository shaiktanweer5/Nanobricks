    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What information do you need from me to start work?

      Share project details such as scope, timeframes, and business challenges you'd like to solve.

    • How long does it take to develop my project?

      We first evaluate the client's project scope, then analyse them to determine how precise our projections were. Once you're glad with the cost and timeframe, you'll need to create a project responsibility and a proposal.Our business analyst will plan this and send it for your review. The proposal would move on to the next stage, development, once everything was resolved.

    • Should I be familiar with the technical details to work with you?

      Definitely not. You do not need to be an expert in software development because we are. As we work on the project, we are prepared to explain all ambiguous moments and the essence of all stages. All you need is a clear vision of the goals you want to achieve with your chosen software. As a result, all you have to do is focus on your needs, and we will work with you to make them a reality.

    • Can the software be upgraded in the future as new technology becomes available?

      Of course! We are always eager to improve, upgrade, and expand our work.

      We work in a short iteration mode and test the work results regularly, taking into consideration all customer preferences and the reaction of the target audience. This approach enables us to provide high-quality products to our customers while meeting deadlines. Our primary focus is quality.
